Society Statistics in Europe

This is a data visualisation regarding European societies. It contains several topics which provides readers a better understanding about Europe. Note: Some countries do not have records in the obtained dataset.

Life Expectancy in Europe 2016

  • Latvia, Lithuania, and Bulgaria have life expectancy below 75 years.
  • Western Europe generally has higher life expectancy than Eastern Europe.

Environmental Satisfaction in Europe 2013

  • Belgium has environmental satisfaction rated medium up to around 70%.
  • Citizens in Bulgaria are unsatisfied about their environment, with 59% of low satisfaction.
  • For most of the country, medium satisfaction takes higher proportions compares to low satisfaction and high satisfaction.

3 Trust Rating Index in Europe 2013

  • Switzerland has both high trust in political and legal system.
  • Generally, Southern Europe has lower trust compare to other subregions.
  • The distribution of bubbles from the same subregion are generally close to each other.

Unemployment Rate & Average Working Hours in Europe 2016

  • It is obvious that Turkey has highest average working hours per week of 46.8 hours.
  • Greece has highest unemployment rate of 23.6%, this is related to the debt crisis began in 2010.
  • Norway has low unemployment rate as low average working hours compare to other countries.
  • Overall, the average working hours is around 35 to 40 per week.
